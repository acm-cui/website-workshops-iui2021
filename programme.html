CUI@IUI: Theoretical and Methodological Challenges in Intelligent Conversational User Interface Interactions

Workshop at the 2021 ACM Intelligent User Interface (IUI) Conference

Programme

The workshop will last for 4hrs. Programme details will be posted closer to the event. We aremaking plans for both physical and virtual hosting of the workshop, should COVID travel restrictions prevent a physical workshop being held.



Provisional Format

  • Introductions from organisers and participants
  • Short position statements
  • Short paper presentations
  • Break out sessions to discuss work presented
  • Short Break
  • All-hands discussion about next steps for the community
